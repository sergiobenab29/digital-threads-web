About the Event

Digital Threads is an immersive event that explores the intersection of technology and fashion through 3D design software. Attendees will learn how virtual tools are transforming the way fashion is created, presented, and worn. This event is perfect for aspiring designers, tech enthusiasts, and anyone curious about the future of wearable innovation.

What to expect

Here's a glimpse of the knowledge you will gain:

  • Live Software Demos: Witness expert-led, step-by-step sessions on the leading 3D apparel design platforms, focusing on speed, accuracy, and photorealistic rendering techniques.
  • Talks on Digitization: Hear from industry pioneers about the essential transition to digital workflows, the sustainability impacts of 3D, and the future of virtual clothing in the metaverse.
  • Collaborative Design Challenges: Connect with a global community of designers and technologists, participating in virtual challenges to apply new skills and build your professional network.

Event Schedule

FRIDAY

3D Fashion Software Introduction

Discover how cutting-edge 3D fashion software is revolutionizing the design process. This session introduces the tools that allow designers to create virtual garments, simulate fabric movement, and visualize collections before a single thread is sewn. Perfect for beginners, this talk lays the foundation for digital creativity in fashion.

SATURDAY

Digital Design Programs

Dive into the world of digital design platforms used by top fashion houses and indie creators alike. Learn the differences between programs like CLO3D, Browzwear, and Adobe Substance, and how each one supports different stages of the design pipeline. From sketch to runway-ready render.

SUNDAY

Career Paths and Virtual Futures

Explore the evolving landscape of fashion careers in the digital age. This session highlights how virtual design, augmented reality, and AI are reshaping roles in the industry — from digital stylists and virtual merchandisers to 3D modelers and fashion technologists. Learn what skills are in demand, how to build a future-ready portfolio, and where innovation is opening doors for creative professionals.
